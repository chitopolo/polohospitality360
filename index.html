Polo Hospitality 360 LLC

Professional Hospitality Support Services (B2B)

Polo Hospitality 360 LLC is a Wyoming-registered limited liability company providing specialized B2B hospitality support to hotel partners abroad. We do not sell physical products, manage inventory, or conduct retail sales. Our business focuses exclusively on contracted professional services.

  • Coordination and management of online hotel reservations
  • Payment reconciliation and processing with OTAs (e.g., Booking.com, Expedia)
  • International guest communication and support
  • Financial reporting and performance analytics for hotels
  • Funds distribution per service agreements: commission retention, international expenses, and remittance of remaining balances to hotel clients

Services are delivered remotely from the United States and governed by formal service agreements with each client. Pricing and commissions are defined contractually based on booking volumes and agreed service levels. All client relationships are B2B and hotels are verified through a direct contractual process.

About / What We Do

Polo Hospitality 360 LLC is a U.S.-based hospitality management support company (registered in Wyoming) providing reservation coordination, OTA payment reconciliation, international guest communication, and transparent financial reporting for hotel partners. Our operations are exclusively B2B under written service agreements.

We currently operate under an exclusive service agreement with a single hotel partner and may extend our services to other international hotels in the future. All funds received are handled according to the contract: we retain our agreed commission, cover OTA and international service costs, and transfer remaining balances to the hotel client.

Both Polo Hospitality 360 LLC and each hotel client remain legally independent and fully compliant with their respective local tax and legal obligations. Our services are delivered entirely from the United States without physical inventory, shipping, or retail activity.
